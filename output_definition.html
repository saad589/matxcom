The output file generated by running MATXCOM provides data on photon interactions and material properties. Each column in the file is detailed as follows, along with corresponding description and unit of measurement:

Column Header

Description

Unit

E

Photon energy

MeV

MAC

Mass attenuation coefficient

cm2/g

LAC

1/cm

HVL

Half-value layer

cm

TVL

Tenth-value layer

cm

MFP

Mean free path

cm

NEFF

Effective electron density

e/g

CEFF

Effective conductivity

S/m

ACS

Atomic cross-section

cm2/g

ECS

Electronic cross-section

cm2/g

ZEFF

Effective atomic number

R

Ratio of incoherent scattering cross-section to total cross-section

ZEQ

Equivalent atomic number

a, b, c, d, Xk

Geometric Progression (G-P) fitting parameters for Energy Absorption and Exposure Buildup Factors (EABF and EBF). Columns are grouped into two banks for EABF and EBF, respectively.

For further assistance or questions about interpreting the output, refer to the associated publication or contact the author.
